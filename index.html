About the Studio

Design rooted
in intention

Chu Studio is an independent design practice based in Taiwan, working at the intersection of visual identity, typography, and spatial thinking. We believe the most enduring design is built on restraint, clarity, and cultural resonance.

Every project begins with deep listening — understanding not just what a brand looks like, but what it stands for and who it speaks to. The result is work that feels inevitable.
